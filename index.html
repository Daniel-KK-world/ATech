Common Questions

Get clear answers about our development process before starting your project.

How do you determine project costs?

After our initial consultation, I provide a detailed proposal with fixed-price quotes based on your specific requirements. Costs consider development time, technical complexity, and any third-party services needed. Most MVP projects range between $5,000-$15,000.

What's included in your development process?

Every project includes strategy sessions, iterative development with weekly demos, quality assurance testing, deployment, and 30 days of post-launch support. You'll receive all source code and documentation upon completion.

How do you handle project changes or new features?

We document all requirements upfront, but I build in flexibility for adjustments. Small changes are accommodated within the original scope, while significant additions are estimated separately. You'll always approve additional costs before work begins.

What if I need ongoing maintenance after launch?

I offer retainer agreements starting at 5 hours/month for updates, security patches, and minor enhancements. For larger needs, we can discuss dedicated support packages tailored to your traffic and update frequency.

Who owns the code after delivery?

You retain full ownership of all custom code developed for your project. I only request permission to include the work in my portfolio (excluding sensitive business logic). All intellectual property rights transfer to you upon final payment.

What if the project takes longer than estimated?

Fixed-price contracts include buffer time for unexpected challenges. If delays occur due to scope changes, we'll discuss options upfront. For hourly engagements, you're only billed for actual time spent.
